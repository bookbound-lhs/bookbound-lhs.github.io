FULLY BOOKED

The Street, Alabang Town Center, Commerce Avenue, Muntinlupa, 1780 Metro Manila 

Established in 2003, Fully Booked opened its first bookstore branch in Rockwell and has grown to 25 branches nationwide. Known for its comprehensive selection of books under every interest, the Philippine bookstore's signature ambiance has also become a haven for thought tinkerers and readers alike. With over a hundred thousand titles across multiple genres, Fully Booked has captured the loyalty of book lovers of all ages. From Graphic Novels to books on Cookery, Architecture, and Design, anyone who walks in a Fully Booked bookstore with an open mind and a thirst for a good read will ultimately find a story to fall in love with. Fully Booked has expanded to offer one-of-a-kind merchandise to suit the lifestyle of its readers. We offer high-quality stationery, arts and crafts, novelty notebooks, vinyl records, and other collectibles as unique as its patrons

They have a wide selection of books for all ages, comic books, vinyl records, albums, high-end rare to find art materials, artist-grade art materials, and there's even stationery on the bottom level. the books are unwrapped for the customers can choose freely and skim over the books but kept organized and neat. Some escalators take you to each level and chairs for you to sit while reading. There's also a Starbucks and frozen yogurt place inside the book store. The interior decor and murals of the Fullybooked bookstore are well architectured. For Filipino book lovers, comic enthusiasts and music lovers this is a go-to place to shop.

CHAPTERS & PAGES

1359 EDSA-North Ave Terminal, Quezon City, Metro Manila 

Chapters and Pages is the place to be. They offer very cheap prices possible,e for a book. Buying books in chapter and pages is comparable to Instead of one book, you could buy a book or 3 for the same price. They also have really inexpensive school supplies, a lot of stock of pad papers and notebooks. There is a whole area in the store that is dedicated to students. They have a wide selection of books, Any kind of books you are looking for is available in chapters and pages which they sell for a lower price compared to ton other sores. Chapters and pages is a place to visit For any bookworm who has a tight budget or who wants to buy more books. It is the best store to buy from if you ever want to stock up on books and grow your collection for an affordable price
